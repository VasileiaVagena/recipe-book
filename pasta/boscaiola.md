# Pasta boscaiola recipe


## Ingredients

- rigatoni
- pancetta
- peas
- mushrooms
- onions


## Instructions

1. Cook pasta: Boil rigatoni in salted water until al dente. Reserve a little pasta water.
2. Sauté pancetta: In a large pan, cook pancetta until crispy.
3. Add veggies: Add chopped onion, mushrooms, and peas. Sauté until tender.
3. Combine: Toss in cooked rigatoni, a splash of pasta water, and mix well.
